# Undergraduate Lab Portal
The Undergraduate Lab Portal is a centralized platform that simplifies finding and applying for various research positions by undergraduate students- replacing old methods like e-mails, with a structured, transparent, and intelligent system that does it all in the same place. This unified platform connects students and faculty through a structured recruitment system with built-in filtering, management tools, and an intelligent matching algorithm.

# User roles & features
✔ Student:
- Creates and manages a professional research profile, highlighting technical skills
- Browses open lab/research positions
- Applies direvtly through the platform

✔ Professors:
- Post open research projects and lab assistant positions
- Specify required GPA thresholds
- Filter and search applicants by: Skills, GPA, Experience etc.
- Make a digital "roster" of current lab members
- Track applicant history and status

✔ Admin (Department-level oversight)
- Monitors department-wide research engagement
- Tracks research output and participation metrics
- Manage funding and stipend approvals
- Oversee lab safety compliance records
- Generate reports for institutional review
